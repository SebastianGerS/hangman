Uppgift 04 - Hangman

Welcome to the game of Hangman

When you push the Start Game, the computer will select a random word or phrase.
A row of boxes will then appear (one for each letter in the selected word(s))

Your goal is to guess what word has been selected,
you do this by clicking on the text input field and then typing the letter you want to guess on,
followed by pressing enter or hitting the Submit your guess button.

You can also type in the inputfield by clicking the letterbuttons in the game, this sends the input to the inputfield
For every letter you guess the corresponding letterbutton will be highlighted and disabled to help you keep track of your guesses if the guess is correct the letter will apper in the coresponding box above

Note that it's also possible to guess on a complet word or phrase, but because letterbuttons become disabled you need to use your native keyboard if you already have one or more correct letters.

You have a maximum of 5 incorrect guesses before you hang 😈
Good luck!
